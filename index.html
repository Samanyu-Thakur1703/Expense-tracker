The Anatomy of an Expense

Knowledge is wealth. Understanding where your money goes is the first step to financial freedom.

📊

Why Track?

It's the process of recording all your financial outflows. Without tracking, small recurring leakages can secretly drain your entire budget.

🎯

The 50/30/20 Rule

A proven strategy: Allocate 50% for Needs, 30% for Wants, and 20% for Savings. Our dashboard helps visualize this split.

🌱

Fixed vs Variable

Fixed expenses (rent, insurance) rarely change. Variable expenses (dining, shopping) are where you have the most power to cut back.

How to Master the Tracker

Follow these simple steps to gain total control over your financial life.

1

Set Your Monthly Baseline

Start by entering your total monthly budget in the tracker. This creates a hard ceiling so the system can warn you if you overspend.

2

Log Every Transaction

Whenever you earn or spend, log it immediately. Categorizing your transactions accurately is vital for generating the breakdown piecharts.

3

Analyze & Optimize

Use the Filter tools to review specific categories. Look at the generated Statistics Breakdown to find exactly where you can cut back next month.
